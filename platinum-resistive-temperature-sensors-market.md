<p><p>The Platinum Resistive Temperature Sensors market is primarily segmented into Wire-Wound and Thin Film types. Wire-Wound sensors consist of fine platinum wire wrapped around a ceramic or glass core, offering high accuracy and stability for extreme temperatures. Thin Film sensors, on the other hand, feature a thin layer of platinum deposited on a substrate, providing faster response times and compact designs, making them ideal for use in applications with space constraints and rapid temperature changes.</p></p>
