<p><p>Alloy cored wire in metallurgy includes various types, each serving specific purposes in steelmaking and treatment processes. Calcium silicide cored wire (CaSi) enhances deoxidation and desulfurization while improving fluidity. Calcium solid cored wire (Ca) primarily offers effective desulfurization. Pure carbon cored wire (C) is used to adjust carbon content, enhancing material properties. Ferro calcium cored wire (CaFe) provides both calcium and iron, facilitating effective refining. Other variations may include different alloying elements tailored for specialized metallurgical applications.</p></p>
