<p><p>The Stainless Steel Powder for Metal Injection Molding (MIM) market is categorized into three types: Austenitic, Martensitic, and Ferritic grades. Austenitic grades, known for their high corrosion resistance and ductility, are widely used in various applications. Martensitic grades offer higher strength and hardness, making them suitable for tools and cutting applications. Ferritic grades, characterized by their magnetic properties and resistance to oxidation, are used in automotive and decorative applications. Each grade serves distinct needs in the MIM industry.</p></p>
