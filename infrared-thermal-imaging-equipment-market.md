<p><strong>The Infrared Thermal Imaging Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Type</li><li>Quantum Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared thermal imaging equipment market comprises two primary types: Hot Type and Quantum Type. Hot Type thermal imagers use bolometric sensors that detect temperature variations in objects, making them suitable for general-purpose applications such as building inspections and surveillance. Quantum Type thermal imagers, on the other hand, utilize advanced semiconductor technologies to achieve higher sensitivity and resolution, enabling precise detection of minute temperature changes. This makes them ideal for specialized applications in defense, research, and medical diagnostics.</p></p>
