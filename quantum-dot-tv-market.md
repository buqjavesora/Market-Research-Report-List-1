<p><strong>The Quantum Dot TV Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>QLED</li><li>QDEF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Quantum Dot TVs utilize advanced technology to enhance display quality through vibrant colors and improved brightness. Two prominent market types include QLED and QDEF. QLED (Quantum Dot Light Emitting Diode) uses quantum dots in conjunction with an LED backlight, offering impressive color accuracy and high dynamic range. In contrast, QDEF (Quantum Dot Enhancement Film) enhances existing LCD displays by applying a quantum dot film for better color reproduction and brightness without changing the entire panel structure. Both aim to elevate the visual experience.</p></p>
