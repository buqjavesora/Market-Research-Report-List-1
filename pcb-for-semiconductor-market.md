<p><p>In the semiconductor market, PCBs (Printed Circuit Boards) play essential roles in testing and reliability. Load boards facilitate electrical connections during device testing, ensuring proper functionality. Probe cards are used for wafer testing, allowing electrical probing of die to verify performance before packaging. Burn-in boards are specialized PCBs that subject semiconductor devices to prolonged electrical stress at elevated temperatures, helping identify early failures. Together, these boards are critical for quality assurance in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
