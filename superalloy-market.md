Iron-based superalloys offer good mechanical properties and are often utilized in applications requiring less extreme conditions. Cobalt-based superalloys provide outstanding wear resistance and are suitable for applications like gas turbines, where high performance is crucial. Each type serves specific industrial demands and applications.</p></p>
