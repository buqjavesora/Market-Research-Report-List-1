<p><p>Precision reduction gears are specialized gear systems used to reduce speed while increasing torque with high accuracy. The RV precision reduction gears utilize a cycloidal design, providing high efficiency and compactness, making them ideal for robotic applications. Harmonic precision reduction gears, on the other hand, employ a flexible spline design, allowing for smooth operation and minimal backlash, making them suitable for applications requiring precise positioning, such as aerospace and medical devices. Both types cater to demanding performance requirements in various industries.</p></p>
