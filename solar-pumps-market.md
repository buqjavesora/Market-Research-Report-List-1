<p><p>The solar pumps market primarily comprises two types: submersible and surface pumps. Submersible pumps operate underwater, drawing water from deep wells or boreholes, making them ideal for agricultural and rural water supply applications. They feature a robust design to withstand high-pressure environments. Surface pumps, on the other hand, are installed above ground and are used for applications like irrigation and water transfer from rivers or reservoirs. Both types leverage solar energy to enhance sustainability and reduce operational costs.</p></p>
