<p><p>The stainless steel corrugated pipe market includes various connection types: **Flange Connection** uses flanges bolted together for secure, leak-proof joints, ideal for high-pressure systems. **Welding** involves fusing pipes directly, ensuring strong, permanent connections suitable for extreme conditions. **Screw Connection** employs threaded fittings for easy assembly and disassembly, commonly used in low-pressure applications. **Others** encompass specialized methods like compression fittings or quick-connect systems, offering flexibility for unique requirements. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, balancing durability, ease of installation, and operational demands.</p></p>
