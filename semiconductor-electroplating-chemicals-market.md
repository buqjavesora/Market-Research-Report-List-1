<p><ul><li>Pretreatment Agent</li><li>Electroplating Additive</li><li>Post-treatment Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor electroplating chemicals market comprises three key types: pretreatment agents, electroplating additives, and post-treatment agents. Pretreatment agents prepare substrate surfaces for optimal adhesion and coating quality. Electroplating additives enhance the electroplating process by improving deposit characteristics, such as uniformity and surface finish. Post-treatment agents, on the other hand, are used to enhance the properties of the plated layers, including corrosion resistance and durability, ensuring the final product meets stringent performance standards.</p></p>
