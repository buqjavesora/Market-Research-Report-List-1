<p><p>Aniline Point Apparatus is a specialized instrument used to determine the aniline point of hydrocarbons, a critical parameter for various petroleum products. The aniline point signifies the minimum temperature at which equal volumes of aniline and the hydrocarbon can be mixed to form a single homogeneous liquid, indicating aromatic content and compatibility with certain solvents. This test is essential in industries such as petrochemicals, lubricants, and automotive, where the quality and performance of fuels and oils are of utmost importance.</p><p>The Aniline Point Apparatus Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.2% during the forecast period.
