<p><p>The rivet squeezer market consists of three main types: pneumatic, hydraulic, and electric rivet squeezers. Pneumatic rivet squeezers rely on compressed air for operation, offering speed and efficiency in high-volume applications. Hydraulic rivet squeezers utilize hydraulic pressure, providing robust force for heavy-duty tasks, ideal for thick materials. Electric rivet squeezers, powered by electric motors, offer versatility and ease of use with less noise and maintenance. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, enhancing productivity in rivet fastening processes.</p></p>
