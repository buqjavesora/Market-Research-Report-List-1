<p><strong>The Road Graders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rigid Frame Road Grader</li><li>Articulated Frame Road Grader</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The road grader market is primarily categorized into two types: rigid frame road graders and articulated frame road graders. Rigid frame road graders possess a fixed, inflexible structure, providing stability and precision for leveling surfaces. They are ideal for tasks requiring high accuracy. In contrast, articulated frame road graders feature a pivot point between the front and rear sections, enhancing maneuverability and flexibility, making them suitable for varied terrain and complex grading tasks. Both types serve essential roles in construction and maintenance projects.</p></p>
