<p><strong>The Flat Ribbon Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shielded</li><li>Unshielded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flat ribbon cables are categorized into two main types: shielded and unshielded. Shielded flat ribbon cables incorporate a protective layer to prevent electromagnetic interference, making them ideal for sensitive applications where signal integrity is crucial. In contrast, unshielded flat ribbon cables lack this protective layer, resulting in a more cost-effective solution suited for less critical environments. Both types are widely used in electronics, telecommunications, and computer systems, catering to various needs based on interference protection requirements and budget constraints.</p></p>
